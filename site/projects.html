Project Log

Marine logistics support delivered across Nigeria’s offshore fields.

TLS has provided multi-year logistics, security, and vessel management services for offshore energy operators and partners.

Execution Timeline

Selected highlights from 2017 to present.

Key engagements

2020–2021 | Monipulo Abana Field

Holistic marine logistics support with Halliburton/Unitech joint venture, providing 3-4 supply vessels and rig towage services from September to January.

2017–2019 | MT Godshand Tanker Management

Maintained and managed the 12,000 MT tanker vessel for Diamond/Access Bank.

2018–2019 | Nestoil-Whitedove Shipping

Mooring support for crude lifting operations with partner AHTS vessels.

2020–2021 | Century Energy Aje Field

Security surveillance contract with ongoing support in Oyo and Ewo fields from 2022 to date.

2021–2023 | Shelfdrilling Trident 8

Monthly crew change, water, and food supply to the Shelfdrilling rig at Abana field.

2021 & 2023 | Mooring Operations

Supported mooring operations for Century Energy at Aje and Oyo fields.

Ongoing | Conoil Belbop

Monthly crew change, water supply, and food supply for Shelfdrilling Rig SDR.

Ongoing | Century Energy Logistics

Logistics support from naval dockyard to Aje and Oyo fields for offshore food deliveries and crew change, plus security surveillance at Oyo field.

Additional engagements

Support services for Shell at Focados, security escort for General Hydrocarbon in and out Port Harcourt, and offshore vessel support for First Marine Limited.
